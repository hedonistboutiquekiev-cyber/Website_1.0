Cookie Notice

This cookie notice is prepared to inform our website visitors about the technologies used on our website and how visitors can make informed choices about how to manage cookies.

What is a cookie?

Every time you visit our website, we place cookies on your device for different purposes. A cookie is a small text-based file that is downloaded and stored on your computer, mobile or similar device and contains information related to your navigation on the website. For example, they can be used to track which pages you visit on the website, remember the information you entered or preferences such as language settings.

Why do we use them?

We use cookies to provide you with full functionality of the website, customize your user experience, perform analyses and improve our services. Cookies are also used to serve personalized ads on our websites, apps and newsletters via the internet and social media platforms, for example to gain insights into how many people clicked on a Social Media ad from us to visit a page on our website. This information is used to measure and optimize ads on social media.

Who is responsible for placing cookies on our website?

All cookies have a publisher that tells you who the cookie belongs to. Some cookies are placed on the websites by us, these are called "first-party cookies", others are placed on the website by another organization with our permission. These are called "third-party cookies".

How long are cookies stored?

Cookies can be stored on your browser or device for various periods. Temporary cookies, called session cookies, are stored on your device until you close your browser. Persistent cookies have an expiration date and the cookie is deleted when that date passes, when you return to the website that created it.

What types of cookies do we use?

We use four cookie categories: strictly necessary, performance, functional and marketing. Only the last three categories listed require user consent. For strictly necessary cookies, user consent is not required because these cookies ensure that the content of the website is displayed completely and continuously so that you can access the website and have a proper digital browsing and online experience.
