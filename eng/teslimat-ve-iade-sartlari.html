2. Return Conditions and Right of Withdrawal

In accordance with the Law No. 6502 on the Protection of the Consumer and the Regulation on Distance Contracts, our customers have the right to return the purchased product within 14 (fourteen) days from the delivery date without showing any reason and without paying any penalty.

Exercising the Right of Withdrawal

  • To exercise your right of withdrawal, you must clearly notify us within the 14-day period via our email address hello@albaspace.com.tr or our phone number +90 538 778 10 18.
  • Following your notification, you must send the product back to us within the legal period of 10 (ten) days.

Return Conditions (Right of Withdrawal)

  • The product to be returned must be unused, its original packaging must be undamaged, and it must be in a condition suitable for resale.
  • The product must be sent together with all its accessories, any gift items (if applicable), and the original invoice.

Return Shipping Fee (Right of Withdrawal)

  • According to legal regulations, return costs in case of exercising the right of withdrawal generally belong to the seller. In this context, if you send your returns via our contracted shipping company Yurtiçi Kargo using the code [Return Code], the shipping fee will be covered by us.
  • If the consumer chooses a shipping company other than our contracted one for the return, the shipping fee shall be covered by the consumer.
  • If our contracted shipping company does not have a branch in the consumer's location, we will ensure that the product is collected from you without any additional cost.

3. Consumer Rights in Case of Defective Goods

If the delivered product is found to be defective (faulty, damaged), pursuant to Article 11 of the Law on the Protection of the Consumer, you have the following optional rights:

  1. Rescission of Contract: The right to return the product and demand a full refund of the price paid. In this case, the seller is obliged to refund the paid amount immediately.
  2. Replacement with a Non-Defective Equivalent: The right to demand the replacement of the product with a non-defective one, if available. This request must be fulfilled within 30 business days at the latest.
  3. Free Repair: The right to demand the product be repaired free of charge. The repair period is a maximum of 30 business days.
  4. Price Reduction in Proportion to the Defect: The right to keep the product and demand a discount from the sales price in proportion to the defect. In this case, the discount amount is refunded immediately.

Important Notes

  • All costs arising in the case of defective goods (shipping, expertise, etc.) belong to the seller.
  • The consumer can use one of these optional rights. The seller is obliged to fulfill the consumer's preference.
  • These rights are valid for a period of 2 (two) years from the delivery date of the product.
  • Defects that appear within the first 6 (six) months following the delivery of the product are considered to have existed at the date of delivery. In this case, the burden of proving that the defect occurred later belongs to the seller.

4. Products for Which the Right of Withdrawal Cannot Be Exercised

The right of withdrawal cannot be exercised in the following cases:

  • Products prepared in line with the customer's special requests or personal needs.
  • Hygienic products such as underwear, swimwear, bikinis, and earrings.
  • Books, digital content, and computer consumables whose protective elements such as packaging, tape, or seal have been opened after delivery.
  • Products that are perishable or whose expiration date may pass.
  • Services performed instantly in the electronic environment or intangible goods delivered instantly to the consumer.
  • Electronic products such as mobile phones, smartwatches, tablets, and computers whose packaging has been opened (unless they are defective).

5. Dispute Resolution

For disputes you may experience regarding the products and services you have purchased, you can apply to the following authorities to seek your legal rights. The authority to apply to is determined according to the dispute value.

  • Consumer Arbitration Committees: For disputes under 104,000 TL (One hundred and four thousand Turkish Liras) for the year 2024, it is mandatory to apply to the Consumer Arbitration Committee in your place of residence or where the consumer transaction was made.
  • Consumer Courts: For disputes of 104,000 TL (One hundred and four thousand Turkish Liras) and above for the year 2024, applications are made to Consumer Courts. Appeals against Consumer Arbitration Committee decisions are also made to Consumer Courts.
