5. Cookie Policy

Our website uses cookies to improve user experience, operate the site efficiently and conduct marketing activities. In accordance with KVKK, transparency and users' explicit consent are essential for the processing of personal data through cookies.

Cookie Consent Mechanism

On your first visit to our site, you will encounter a cookie banner that informs you about the use of cookies. Through this banner, explicit consent is requested from you for the use of non-essential cookies.

  • Explicit Consent: Your consent is obtained through an active action such as clicking a button like "Accept All". Continuing to use the site does not mean you have consented to the use of cookies.
  • Detailed Selection: You are offered "Reject All" and "Manage Cookies" options. This way, you have the right to choose which cookie categories to allow. Checkboxes for functional, analytical and marketing cookies are not pre-checked.
  • Withdrawal of Consent: You can easily withdraw your consent or change your preferences at any time through the "Cookie Settings" link located in the footer of our site.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for the site to operate securely and perform basic functions (e.g. shopping cart, login). Explicit consent is not sought for these cookies.
  • Functional Cookies: Provides a more personalized experience by remembering your preferences such as language or region.
  • Performance/Analytical Cookies: Helps us improve our service quality by collecting statistical information about how you use the site.
  • Advertising/Marketing Cookies: Used to create profiles based on your interests and show you relevant ads.

For detailed information about all the cookies we use, their purposes, storage periods and the parties they belong to, please review our Cookie Policy.
